Follow the Sirens

Keeping track of the Berliner Feuerwehr activity near you

By Letícia Figueiredo Collado and Xiaohan Wu

Emergency vehicles in Germany are known for their brain-breaking sirens. Their loud and piercing sounds decidely make room for the necessary service to get through - it is impossible not to notice them.

Did you ever wonder where the fire trucks are going? Where in Berlin are all these fires? Are they really all fires?

Follow us in this interactive path to understand some of the fire brigade activity in Berlin near your home, work, school or any other place of interest.
