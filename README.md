<h1 align="center">
  Follow the Sirens
</h1>

<p align="center">
  <strong>Keeping track of the Berliner Feuerwehr activity near you</strong>
</p>

<p align="right">
  <em>By Letícia Figueiredo Collado and Xiaohan Wu</em>
</p>

<br>

This code refers to an interactive path built to fullfil credits in the course Data Journalism (E1493), conducted by prof. Simon Munzert, during the Spring semester of 2026 at the Hertie School of Governance, in Berlin. Access the final product here: https://lefcollado.github.io/Data-bit-1/  

The page was developed using data from the Berliner Feuerwehr available in their [official website](https://www.berliner-feuerwehr.de/service/open-data/) as well as in their [GitHub page](https://github.com/Berliner-Feuerwehr/BF-Open-Data). Data on Berlin's population was gathered from the Amt für Statistik Berlin-Brandenburg's [official page](https://www.statistik-berlin-brandenburg.de/a-i-5-hj).
